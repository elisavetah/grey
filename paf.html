Pastor Jarvis Wash

Jarvis Wash is the Founder and Senior Pastor of the newly birthed The R.E.A.L. Church, Inc. a Real innovative and growing ministry where we are Reaching Equipping Affecting Lives for Christ. He is married to First Lady Elois Wash who supports him in ministry. He served as Lead and Staff Pastor of Empowerment Ministries Church, Rockledge and Orlando FL, respectively, founded by Bishop Kelvin l. Cobaris where he served 12 faithful years. He also served as Youth Pastor of Greater Central Church, Orlando FL under the leadership of Elisha Parris and Bishop Cobaris. Greater Central which was located in the heart of the Holden Heights Community of Orlando, Florida, which was designated by Governor Bush as a "Front Porch" Community - distressed and underdeveloped. Pastor Jarvis Wash received his ministry license and thus began his spiritual and community outreach journey. Pastor Wash has served as a Christian Education Leader, Marriage Counselor and because of his great passion for the Youth, he was then promoted as Youth Pastor. In this active role as Youth Pastor, Pastor Jarvis Wash started the "YOUTH EXPLOSION" which was a monthly service geared toward the youth, their families and the community of Holden Heights which not only expanded the Youth Ministry base at GCC but the entire membership base. GCC became a pinnacle resource within the Holden Heights and other surrounding communities.

Because of past experiences in the streets of Oakland, CA, Pastor Wash felt the need to serve and give back to communities that were filled with prostitutes, pimps and drug dealers. After experiencing a tragic violent death of their son, Brandon in 2004, Pastor Wash founded "THE RELEASE", a yearly evangelism effort to reduce crime. "THE RELEASE" effort canvass disadvantage areas to access the needs of the community by bringing resources and seminars to further educate; bring employment and home ownership opportunities. With this effort, Pastor Wash began to partner with small and local businesses, churches, various Christian artists and entertainers; local law enforcements and community leaders within that area. In 2005, Pastor Wash founded the "NEXT MINISTRIES" which was a mentorship program for boys ages 8-17. Pastor Wash also studied at The International Seminary located in Plymouth, Florida.

Since his tenure here at The R.E.A.L. Church - Rockledge, FL and Brevard County, Pastor Wash have been making great impact within this community. In 2009, Pastor Jarvis became a Pre and Post HIV Counselor. He formed partnerships with local doctors and local HIV and AIDS Testing Centers. He has hosted on numerous occasions HIV and Aids Seminars and also collaborated with O.U.R Community Health Services, Inc. to sponsor the "Guard Your Heart" seminar. Pastor Jarvis sits on various boards such as the Brevard Juvenile Re-entry and "Youth Promise". He headed the "Stop the Killing and Start Living" Movement in Rockledge/Cocoa. He is a certified Chaplain through the Department of Juvenile Justice. He is also the founder of The S.P.O.T (A Special Place for Our Teens) which is a summer and afterschool program that educate, train and equip teenagers by providing life skills and helping them to deter and prevent criminal activities, alcohol, drugs, gangs, and teenage pregnancy.

In 2012, Pastor Wash established Jarvis Marlo Ministries, Inc. (JMM) which is his personal preaching, teaching and motivational speaking ministry geared toward Outreach, Community Collaboration, Leadership and Team Building.
