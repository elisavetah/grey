Our Vision

The R.E.A.L. Church Inc. first and ultimate goal is to continually be soul oriented demonstrated by winning the lost for Jesus Christ, therefore, increasing the body of Christ, healing for those Christians that have been hurt in past ministries, therefore strengthening the body, mentoring and educating our membership and surrounding communities, therefore empowering the body. The R.E.A.L. Church will not be just another church in the community but will be the "Thriving" Community Church of Brevard County, Florida.

Our Purpose

The very existence and purpose of the R.E.A.L. Church is Reaching, Equipping and Affecting Lives for Jesus Christ through aggressive outreaches, annual soul winning campaigns, bible teaching and mentoring.

Our Mission

To continue providing the church and community with outreaches, educational and mentorship programs.
